About us.

Kerala Blockchain Academy Innovation Club (KBAIC) is a novel initiative by Kerala Blockchain Academy (KBA) to help students from academic institutions across the country to explore deep into the potential of the blockchain technology. KBA-CET aims to tap into this disruptive technology and make the students equipped and industry-ready for blockchain applications. Dr. K Sunil Kumar, Asst Professor Department of Mechanical Engg is the coordinator for the KBAIC.
